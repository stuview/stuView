About StuView

StuView is an online practice interview application designed to assist students in preparing for real-world interviews. The main goal of StuView is to be able to simulate not only HR question interviews, but also technical question interviews.

Instructors can create two types of interviews. One type is practice interviews, which can be taken as many times as a student would like, and will not be uploaded and sent to an industry assessor for review, but will be available for download to the student to review themselves. The second type is a reviewed interview, which will be uploaded and sent to an industry assessor for review and feedback when the student has completed the interview, or the time has exipired, whichever comes first. Industry assessors will submit feedback on the interview, then students will be able to view that feedback to learn what they did well or what they can improve on.
